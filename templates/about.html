About EcoYugam

Transforming India's environmental landscape through integrated circular economy solutions that eliminate waste, restore ecosystems, and create sustainable livelihoods while building partnerships across government, communities, and industry.

Our Mission Meet Our Team

Our Mission

To revolutionize India's waste management ecosystem by implementing circular economy principles that transform waste into valuable resources, create green livelihoods, and restore environmental health through innovative technology and community collaboration.

Circular Economy Implementation

Designing systems where waste becomes input for new products and processes.

Multi-Stakeholder Collaboration

Building partnerships between government, industry, and communities.

Environmental Restoration

Rehabilitating ecosystems and water bodies impacted by pollution.

Our Vision

A waste-free India where resources circulate continuously, communities thrive in clean environments, and economic prosperity aligns with ecological balance.

Our Core Values

Focusing on solutions that deliver measurable environmental benefits and can be scaled across diverse contexts.

Our Journey

From a small initiative to a nationwide movement for environmental transformation.

2018

Foundation & Pilot Program

EcoYugam was founded with a pilot program in three communities, focusing on segregation at source and community composting.

2019

Technology Integration

Launched our first digital platform for waste tracking and introduced IoT-enabled smart bins in partnership with municipal corporations.

2020

National Expansion

Expanded operations to 12 states, established partnerships with UNDP and NITI Aayog, and launched the E-Waste to Resources initiative.

2021

Circular Economy Framework

Developed India's first comprehensive circular economy framework for urban waste management, adopted by 5 major cities.

2022-Present

Scaling Impact

Currently operating in 18 states, diverting over 12,000 tons of waste monthly, and creating 1,850+ green jobs across India.
