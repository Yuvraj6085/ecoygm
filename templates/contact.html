Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about EcoYugam and our programs.

How can my organization partner with EcoYugam?

We welcome partnerships with corporations, NGOs, government bodies, and educational institutions. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@ecoyugam.org or use the contact form above to discuss potential collaboration.

Do you offer volunteering opportunities?

Yes! We have various volunteering opportunities across our programs. You can apply through our website or contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteers@ecoyugam.org for more information.

How can I implement EcoYugam's programs in my community?

We work with communities across India to implement our waste management and sustainability programs. Contact our community engagement team to discuss how we can bring our initiatives to your area.

Do you provide educational resources or workshops?

Yes, we offer educational workshops, training programs, and resources for schools, colleges, and community groups. Contact our education team at education@ecoyugam.org for more details.

Don't see your question here? Contact us directly and we'll be happy to help.
