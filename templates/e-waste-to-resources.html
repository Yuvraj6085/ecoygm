India's E-Waste Challenge

India is the third-largest generator of e-waste globally, with over 3.2 million tonnes produced annually. Only 10% of this waste is processed through formal channels, leading to:

  • Environmental Pollution: Toxic materials leaching into soil and water
  • Health Hazards: Informal recycling exposes workers to dangerous substances
  • Resource Loss: Valuable materials worth ₹4,500+ Cr lost annually
  • Data Security Risks: Improper disposal compromises personal information

E-waste is growing at 31% annually in India - one of the fastest rates in the world
