Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and how we work with clients.

How long does it typically take to implement a waste management system?

Implementation timelines vary based on project scope and complexity. Simple community programs can be operational in 2-3 months, while comprehensive municipal systems may take 6-12 months for full implementation.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?

Yes, we offer various support packages including monitoring, optimization, and staff training to ensure long-term success. Our standard implementation includes 6 months of post-implementation support.

What metrics do you use to measure success?

We track multiple KPIs including waste diversion rates, recycling percentages, cost savings, carbon emission reductions, job creation, and community engagement levels. All metrics are customized to your specific goals.

Can you help with regulatory compliance?

Absolutely. Our team includes experts in environmental regulations who can ensure your operations comply with all local, state, and national requirements, including Extended Producer Responsibility rules.
