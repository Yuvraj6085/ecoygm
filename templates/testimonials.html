Voices of Impact

Hear directly from community members, partners, government officials, and beneficiaries about their experiences with EcoYugam's programs and the difference they've made.

Read Stories Share Your Story

Priya Sharma

Community Leader, Delhi

"The Waste Recycling Initiative transformed our neighborhood. Before EcoYugam, we had overflowing bins and waste everywhere. Now, we've reduced landfill waste by 75% and created income opportunities for local women through the recycling program. Our community is cleaner, healthier, and more united."

Participant since 2021

Rajesh Kumar

Municipal Commissioner

"EcoYugam's smart waste management system has been a game-changer for our city. The IoT-enabled bins and optimized collection routes reduced our operational costs by 30% while increasing recycling rates significantly. Their data-driven approach provides insights we never had before. A true public-private partnership success story."

Partner since 2020

Anil Mehta

CSR Head, GreenTech Industries

"Our partnership with EcoYugam on the E-Waste to Resources program has been exceptional. They helped us establish a compliant and efficient system for managing our electronic waste, creating both environmental and social impact. The transparency in their reporting gives us confidence that our CSR investment is making a real difference."

Partner since 2022

Sunita Patel

Waste Collector turned Entrepreneur

"I used to work as a waste picker in unsafe conditions. EcoYugam's training program gave me the skills to start my own small recycling business. Now I employ 5 people from my community and process 500kg of plastic monthly. My children are in school, and we have a stable home. This program changed my life completely."

Beneficiary since 2019

Dr. Amit Joshi

Environmental Scientist, UNDP

"EcoYugam's approach to circular economy implementation is exemplary. Their multi-stakeholder model effectively bridges gaps between government, industry, and communities. The data they collect provides valuable insights for policy development. We're proud to partner with them on scaling circular economy solutions across India."

Partner since 2020

Rahul Verma

College Student & Volunteer

"Volunteering with EcoYugam's Green Earth Plantation Drive was one of the most rewarding experiences of my life. Not only did I help plant 500+ trees, but I also learned about ecosystem restoration and community engagement. The training sessions were eye-opening, and I've since started an environmental club at my college."

Volunteer since 2022
