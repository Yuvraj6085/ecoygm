The Urban Waste Challenge

Indian cities generate over 150,000 tons of municipal solid waste daily, with collection efficiency often below 70%. Traditional waste management systems face multiple challenges:

  • Inefficient Collection: Fixed routes regardless of bin fill levels
  • Bin Overflow: Public health hazards and environmental pollution
  • High Operational Costs: Fuel, labor, and maintenance expenses
  • Data Deficiency: Limited insights for planning and optimization

How Smart Bins Work

Our IoT-enabled smart bins transform waste management through real-time monitoring and data-driven optimization.

Real-time Monitoring

Ultrasonic sensors continuously monitor fill levels and transmit data via IoT networks.

Data Transmission

Secure cloud connectivity ensures real-time data flow to our central dashboard.

Optimized Routes

AI algorithms create efficient collection routes based on actual bin fill levels.

Analytics & Insights

Comprehensive analytics help cities plan better and reduce operational costs.
